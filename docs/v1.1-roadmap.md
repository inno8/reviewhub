## Decision log

- **Why ship `/handleiding` BEFORE weight customization?** Schools that
  flex weights without understanding the Bayesian math will misuse the
  feature. The doc grounds them.
- **Why not ship CodeT5 in v1?** Heavy compute, model selection, and
  fine-tune cost. Defer until the simpler Dolos integration is
  validated and we have a clear use case beyond "shiny ML thing."
- **Why split admin/ops dashboards into their own clusters?** The
  user-facing app dashboards (student/teacher) are the demo focus. The
  admin/ops surfaces are operationally important but sell themselves
  as "we can run a school" vs "we can grade code." Different beats.
- **Why parallelize G and H with the rest?** Both clusters are mostly
  CRUD UI on top of existing endpoints. They don't block product
  features and a second engineer can pick them up cleanly.
- **Why is Multi-Opdracht earlier than Help Requests despite both
  being "model expansion"?** Help Requests scopes to an Assignment, so
  it depends on Multi-Opdracht having shipped first.

## Future (v1.2+) — explicitly NOT in v1.1

These items came up during v1.1 scoping but were deliberately deferred:

- **Per-school custom rubric extensions** beyond the 6 Crebo werkprocessen
  (full custom criteria with their own LLM prompt tuning). Defer until
  we've validated grading quality at scale with the locked-Crebo +
  weight-flex pattern.
- **LLM-graded backfill** (full LLM analysis of historical PRs).
  Cost-prohibitive (~€5/student × 100 students per cohort = €500/month).
  Layer 1 deterministic backfill in v1.1 is the right starting point.
- **Help Requests with DM-style threading**. v1.1 ships one teacher
  response per request. Threaded conversations belong in Slack/Teams,
  not in LEERA.
- **Help Requests email notifications** (default off). v1.1 is in-app
  only; email opt-in is a per-user setting deferred to v1.2.
- **Cross-cohort Assignments** (one opdracht shared between klassen).
  Real use case for capstone projects but adds permissioning
  complexity. v1.5+.
- **Per-PR LLM upgrade tier** (school admin can pay extra to re-grade
  one specific PR with Opus). Niche; defer until at least one paying
  customer asks for it.
- **Interactive grading-math calculator** on `/handleiding` ("change the
  weights, see Eindniveau change"). Amazing UX but adds frontend
  complexity. v2 onboarding feature.
